## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="book" title="How do I reset my password?">
    To reset your password, click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="user" title="Can I enroll in multiple courses at once?">
    Yes, you can enroll in as many courses as you like. However, we recommend focusing on one or two courses at a time to ensure better learning outcomes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="check" title="What happens if I fail a test?">
    If you fail a test, you can retake it after reviewing the course material. Your teacher may also provide additional resources to help you improve your understanding.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="How do I contact support?">
    You can reach our support team via email at **support@yourlearningapp.com**, by phone at **+1 (123) 456-7890**, or through the live chat feature available 24/7 within the app .
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="gear" title="How do teachers create courses?">
    Teachers can create courses by navigating to the **Teacher Dashboard**, selecting **Create Course**, and adding details such as the course title, description, and multimedia content like videos or PDFs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
